Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

CrickFlow (“we”, “the app”) is operated for cricket scoring and live streaming in Sri Lanka. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information we collect

  • Account: Email, display name, phone number (if you use phone sign-in), and profile photo from Google Sign-In.
  • Cricket data: Teams, players, match scores, tournaments, and stream settings you enter.
  • Device: FCM token for match notifications; camera/microphone only when you start a live stream.

How we use data

To provide scoring, live overlays, tournaments, notifications, and optional YouTube/custom RTMP streaming. We do not sell your personal data.

Storage & security

Data is stored in Google Firebase (project crickflow-b06bc) with authentication and security rules. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure.

Third parties

  • Google (Authentication, Firebase)
  • YouTube / RTMP providers when you choose to broadcast

Your choices

You can delete your account in the app: Settings → Delete Account. This removes your profile, linked player record, notifications, and Firebase sign-in. Matches and teams you created may remain visible to other users until an organizer removes them.

You may also contact us to request deletion of additional data.

Contact
